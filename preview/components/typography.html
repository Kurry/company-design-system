Body Text

Large body text for emphasis and important content. This size works well for introductory paragraphs and key messages.

Medium body text for standard content. This is the most commonly used text size for article content and descriptions.

Regular body text for general content. This size provides optimal readability for most text content.

Small text for captions, labels, and secondary information.
